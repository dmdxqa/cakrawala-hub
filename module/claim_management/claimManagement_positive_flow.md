# Claim Management Workflow

## Overview

Manages warranty claims that have been approved by PGI. Tracks approved claims in the Accepted Claim list, allowing PGI to generate consolidated claims compiled by service territory. Once generated, claims are consolidated into a single claim number per month and removed from the Accepted Claim list. Provides detailed breakdown of claim fees, tax information, and linked work orders.

## Workflow

### View Accepted Claims

1. Navigate to **Claim Management** menu
2. Open **Accepted Claim** tab
3. View list of approved warranty claims waiting to be generated by PGI
4. Verify the following information is displayed:
   - **WO Number** — Work order associated with the claim
   - **Service Territory** — Service center territory
   - **Status** — Claim status (Close)
   - **Claim Submitted** — Date and time claim was submitted
5. Click **Action** button to view claim details
6. Verify **Detail Claim** page is open

### Generate Claim _(Only performed by Superadmin and PGI)_

1. Navigate to **Claim Management** menu
2. Open **Generate Claim** tab
3. Click **Generate Claim** button
4. Verify the system automatically:
   - Compiles accepted claims by service territory
   - Groups claims into a single claim number per month
   - Removes compiled claims from the Accepted Claim list
   - Adds generated claims to the Generate Claim list
5. Verify the following information is displayed for generated claims:
   - **Claim Number** — Unique claim identifier
   - **Service Territory** — Compiled service territory
   - **Period** — Month and year of claim generation (e.g., 05/2026)
   - **Last Updated** — Date and time of generation
   - **Generate By** — User who initiated the claim generation

### View Claim Detail

1. Access to **Claim Management**
2. Navigate to **Accepted Claim** or **Generate Claim** tab
3. Click **Action** button or claim number
4. Verify **Detail Claim** page is open and displays:

### Export Claims _(Only performed by Superadmin and PGI)_

1. Navigate to **Claim Management**
2. Open **Generate Claim** tab
3. Click **Export** button
4. Verify claim data is exported to file format (CSV/Excel)
5. Verify exported data includes all claim records and details
6. Verify file is downloaded to user's device
